Mono-Alphabetic Substitution Tool

This tool allows you to plug in you own text and do mono-alphabetic substitution encryption/decryption either randomly, or based on your own key. You do not have to give a complete key for it to work- whatever letters you do provide a key for will be replaced and in lowercase, whatever you don't will remain the same and in uppercase. See the tutorial on frequency analysis for more information on the topic.
