She spent four years at the University of Virginia where she studied computer science, interned for the FBI, and ate a concerning amount of softserve, only to become lactose intolerant in her final semester. She has since moved back to Richmond, VA to further pursue a career in computer science and attempt to regain a tolerance for lactose.
